Top 10 Scenic Drives in and Around Tangier

Tangier, a vibrant city in northern Morocco, offers an eclectic mix of history, culture, and breathtaking landscapes. One of the best ways to explore Tangier and its surroundings is by car, allowing you to enjoy the scenic beauty at your own pace. Here are the top 10 scenic drives you shouldn't miss when visiting Tangier:

1. Tangier to Cap Spartel

Distance: 14 km Highlights: Cap Spartel Lighthouse, panoramic ocean views, Hercules Caves The drive from Tangier to Cap Spartel is short but incredibly rewarding. As you approach the lighthouse, you'll be treated to stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean. Don't miss the Hercules Caves, where mythology and natural beauty intertwine.

2. Tangier to Asilah

Distance: 46 km Highlights: Coastal views, charming medina, art murals A drive along the Atlantic coast to the picturesque town of Asilah is a must. The route offers beautiful ocean views and leads to a town known for its whitewashed buildings and vibrant art scene. Spend some time exploring the medina and its famous murals.

3. Tangier to Chefchaouen

Distance: 113 km Highlights: Rif Mountains, blue-washed streets, vibrant markets Chefchaouen, also known as the Blue Pearl, is nestled in the Rif Mountains. The drive offers spectacular mountain scenery and, once you arrive, you'll be enchanted by the town's blue-painted streets and laid-back atmosphere.

4. Tangier to Tetouan

Distance: 60 km Highlights: Historic medina, Andalusian architecture, lively souks The road to Tetouan winds through lush valleys and rolling hills. Tetouan's medina is a UNESCO World Heritage site, and its Andalusian influence is evident in the architecture and cultural vibrancy of the city.

5. Tangier to Oued Laou

Distance: 120 km Highlights: Coastal landscapes, pristine beaches, fishing villages This coastal drive takes you through some of the most serene and untouched parts of northern Morocco. Oued Laou is a quiet fishing village where you can relax on pristine beaches and enjoy fresh seafood.

6. Tangier to Larache

Distance: 84 km Highlights: Ancient ruins of Lixus, river views, local markets The drive to Larache offers a mix of coastal and river views. Visit the ancient Phoenician and Roman ruins of Lixus, situated on a hill overlooking the Loukkos River, and explore the vibrant local markets of Larache.

7. Tangier to Ksar el-Kebir

Distance: 110 km Highlights: Countryside vistas, historical sites, local culture Travel inland to the town of Ksar el-Kebir. The journey takes you through the Moroccan countryside, offering glimpses of rural life and historical sites such as the Battle of Alcácer Quibir.

8. Tangier to Fnideq

Distance: 64 km Highlights: Mediterranean coastline, beach resorts, shopping Fnideq is located near the Spanish enclave of Ceuta and offers a different coastal experience along the Mediterranean. The drive is scenic, with beach resorts and opportunities for shopping in local markets.

9. Tangier to M’Diq

Distance: 75 km Highlights: Marina, seafood restaurants, watersports M'Diq is a coastal town known for its marina and vibrant seafood restaurants. The drive along the Mediterranean coast provides stunning sea views, and M'Diq offers various watersport activities.

10. Tangier to Jebel Musa

Distance: 30 km Highlights: Mountain views, hiking trails, panoramic vistas For those who enjoy a bit of adventure, a drive to Jebel Musa offers breathtaking mountain views and hiking opportunities. This scenic drive provides panoramic vistas of both the Mediterranean Sea and the surrounding landscapes.

Tips for a Scenic Drive in Tangier Plan Ahead: Ensure you have a detailed map or GPS, especially for longer drives. Fuel Up: Make sure your car has enough fuel before setting out, as gas stations can be sparse in rural areas. Stay Safe: Follow local driving rules and be cautious on winding roads, especially in mountainous regions. Pack Essentials: Bring water, snacks, and a first aid kit, and ensure your phone is fully charged. Enjoy the Journey: Take your time to stop, explore, and take in the beautiful scenery. Exploring Tangier and its surroundings by car is a fantastic way to discover the diverse landscapes and rich cultural heritage of northern Morocco. Each of these scenic drives offers a unique glimpse into the beauty of the region, making your trip truly memorable.
